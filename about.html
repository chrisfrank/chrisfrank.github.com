The Nepotist makes music the Village Voice calls “moving alt-soul.” Frontmen Chris and Hayden Frank, brothers, grew up in Ithaca, NY, playing in separate bands. They started the Nepotist in New York City in 2012. Since releasing a series of session videos this spring, they’ve been hard at work on their debut LP, due out in January 2015.

Cheryl Dunn took the black and white photos on this site. Lambkini took the one in color.

The inimitable Mike Cook made our logo.
