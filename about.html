The Nepotist is an alternative-soul band in New York City.

Hayden Frank

Bass, Singing

Chris Frank

Guitar, Singing

Jacob Colin Cohen

Drums, Singing

Chris and Hayden are brothers, originally from Ithaca, NY. Chris is older. They grew up playing music separately, and started The Nepotist as a duo in 2012. Jacob joined in 2014 and transformed them into a band.

Cheryl Dunn took the black and white photos on this site. Lambkini took the one in color.

The inimitable Mike Cook made our logo.
