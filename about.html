The Nepotist is an alternative-soul band in New York City.

Chris and Hayden Frank are brothers, originally from Ithaca, NY. Chris is older. They grew up playing music separately, and started The Nepotist as a duo in 2012. Jacob Colin Cohen joined in 2014 and transformed them into a band.

Cheryl Dunn took the black and white photos on this site. Lambkini took the one in color.

The inimitable Mike Cook made our logo.
